Text Styles

Normal text, with bold, italic, and bold italic. ~~Strikethrough~~ is useful for change logs. Inline code for identifiers, npm run build, or variables like FOO=bar. Highlight ==important text== with <mark> in HTML: this is highlighted. Superscript: 2^10^ and Subscript: H~2~O (may require HTML: H2O). Paragraphs & Line Breaks

A paragraph is made of one or more lines of text separated by a blank line.

Line breaks occur when you end a line with two spaces. Or configure your renderer to treat single newlines as breaks.
